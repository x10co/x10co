Methodology

Weighted Poisson Model

Maximum-likelihood Poisson regression per league. Matches weighted by recency using exponential decay (half-life 60 days) — last month counts ~4× more than 4 months ago. Home advantage estimated per league from data.

SPI Ratings

Inspired by FiveThirtyEight's Soccer Power Index (Nate Silver, 2009).

  • OFF: expected goals vs avg team, neutral field
  • DEF: expected goals conceded vs avg team
  • SPI: % of available points (W=3,D=1,L=0) vs avg opponent

Market SPI + Gap

Derived from season-average market odds (de-vigged). Converted to SPI-equivalent. Gap = Model − Market highlights potential inefficiencies where our model disagrees with collective market wisdom.

Match Predictor

Given two teams' weighted attack/defense params + home advantage: builds a full Poisson score matrix with ~9% diagonal draw inflation. Outputs 1X2, Asian Handicap lines, Over/Under, and projected score.

Closing Line Value (CLV)

CLV = Model probability − Market probability. Positive CLV means the model sees more value than the market. Historically, sustained positive CLV is associated with long-run profitability.
